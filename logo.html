My Logo

Concept

This logo is built from the essence of my name, Cesare Ghiotto, with the initials transformed into a clean, stylized mark. Its oval, organic shape reflects natural imperfection and a connection to the world around us. As a self-taught creator, my inspirations come from pop culture, luxury, and the automotive world, with a particular influence from the design language of Pagani, which guided the moodboard behind the final form.
